Form Title: Legal Questionnaire - Personal Injury Claim

Form Reference: CSS-HSA-101-LQ

Version: 1.0

Purpose: To gather comprehensive information from claimants pursuing personal injury claims, ensuring all relevant details are captured for legal proceedings.

Who should complete this: The injured party (claimant) or their authorized representative

Estimated completion time: 45-60 minutes

Data Protection: All information provided is processed in accordance with GDPR and UK data protection laws. Your data will only be shared with legal professionals, medical experts, and insurers as necessary for your claim.

Need Help? Contact your HSQE Consultant: Jeremy Bromley

‚ÑπÔ∏è Help & Guidance

How to Complete This Form

  1. Work through each tab in order - The form is divided into 10 sections covering different aspects of your claim
  2. Be as detailed as possible - The more information you provide, the better your legal representative can assist you
  3. Save regularly - Your progress is automatically saved to your browser, but you can also save manually
  4. Gather supporting documents - Have medical records, payslips, and receipts ready for reference
  5. Answer honestly - All information must be accurate. False information can harm your claim
  6. Seek help if needed - Contact your HSQE Consultant if you're unsure about any questions

Important Tips

  • Fields marked with * are required
  • Take your time - accuracy is more important than speed
  • Keep copies of all supporting documents
  • Declare all pre-existing conditions, even if unrelated
  • Update the form if your circumstances change
